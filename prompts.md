# 📝 Structured Prompts — Glamour Touch Salon

---

## Prompt 1 — Homepage Copy

You are a professional website copywriter specializing in local businesses.

Write homepage website copy for a men's salon called "Glamour Touch Salon" 
located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu.

The copy should include:
1. A strong headline (value proposition)
2. A sub-headline (who it's for + benefit)
3. A short intro paragraph (3-4 lines)

Tone: Confident, fresh, and modern — targeted at men aged 18-40.
Language: Simple English, easy to read.
Goal: Make visitors feel this is the best men's salon in Madurai.

---

## Prompt 2 — Services Page

You are a professional website copywriter.

Write a Services page for "Glamour Touch Salon", a men's salon in Madurai.

List 5 services with:
- Service name
- One line description
- Key benefit for the customer

Tone: Clean, confident, and friendly.
Format: Each service as a short card-style block.

---

## Prompt 3 — CTA Section

Write a Call-to-Action section for "Glamour Touch Salon" men's salon 
in Madurai.

Include:
1. A booking CTA with urgency
2. A trust-building line
3. Location/contact nudge

Tone: Friendly but persuasive.
Keep it short and punchy.
